title=Conditional-Speech
description=[h3]This mod generates speech utilizing a number of conditions as well as some randomization to create dynamic dialogue.[/h3]
description=
description=Note:[list]
description=[*] Color of speech can be changed in the in-game menu under "multiplayer".
description=[*] The game will simulate volume by grayscaling your speech. Pick a vibrant color as to tell the difference in volume.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]This mod includes stock phrases for all of the moodles as well as some events.
description=All of them can be toggled in the menu.[/b]
description=Additionally, the mod applies a dynamic filter on chat depending on moodle states.
description=Being drunk, scared, or in pain can make you scream out or be hard to understand.
description=
